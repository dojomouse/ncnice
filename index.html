A place to stay.

CÃ©cile is at pains to point out that our wedding is not in Nice. It is in Villefranche sur Mer, which about half an hour from the centre of Nice. By foot. By car (or taxi) it's about 5 minutes.

It's a beautiful place - a seaside village in itself. But Nice is also as its name suggests, and almost as convenient. The waterfront spots in Villefranche are usually booked out long in advance, and if you stay further up the hill you're a bit further from the buzz. We'd suggest staying in Nice - there are tons of places centrally located. Especially if you arrive earlier in the week, Nice has the most options for exploring. But they're (basically) the same place, so just find somewhere that suits you.

Wherever you decide to stay, booking.com and airbnb seem to have a great range of options.
