Presents

Tradifex was first conceived in mid 2012, born of a frustration with manual trade optimization methods.

In the pursuit of a more efficient and more profitable trading strategy, Nick Butcher (Tradifex's founder) had written a number of option chain analysis functions. These recommended the most profitable options strategy, working off the user's broader forecasts of future stock price movements.

The functions worked well... the only problem was that it was such a pain to use them that they spent most of their time gathering dust, while Nick's trade strategy suffered.
Nick eventually decided to implement them in a web framework to make life easier.

This decision lead to a chain of discoveries.

  • It's not enough to have a strategy, you also need to track it.
  • The interface for setting and tracking needs to be clear and as effortless as possible.
  • That philosophy should actually apply to your portfolio in general.
  • Even simple trade strategy and portfolio monitoring is such a hassle that most 'retail investors' ignore it altogether
